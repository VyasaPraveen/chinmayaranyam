A Temple of Learning

Harihara Vidyalaya, nestled within the serene campus of Chinmayaranyam, is a premier educational institution that integrates modern academics with India's timeless cultural values.

Founded under the vision of Chinmaya Mission's commitment to holistic education, the Vidyalaya nurtures students to become intellectually competent, morally upright, and socially responsible individuals — true to the motto of Chinmaya Vidya Peeth.

Chinmaya Vision Programme (CVP)

• • •

The Chinmaya Vision Programme (CVP) is an integrated curriculum that develops the child's physical, mental, intellectual, and spiritual dimensions.

Devotion

Daily prayers, bhajan sessions, and celebration of festivals foster a strong spiritual foundation in every student.

Patriotism

National pride and respect for India's rich cultural heritage are instilled through various programs and activities.

Service

Community service projects, environmental awareness, and compassion for all beings are integral to the CVP curriculum.

Art & Culture

Traditional dance, music, drama, and arts are celebrated to preserve and promote India's cultural treasures.

Sports & Fitness

Physical education, yoga, and competitive sports ensure holistic development of mind and body.

Critical Thinking

Students are encouraged to question, explore, and develop independent thinking through interactive learning methods.
