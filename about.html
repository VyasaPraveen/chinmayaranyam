Our Story

A Legacy of Wisdom & Service

Chinmayaranyam, nestled in the sacred environs near Tirupati, is a spiritual haven under the umbrella of Chinmaya Mission. Established and developed by Pujya Shri Mataji Sarada Priyananda, this ashram has been a beacon of Vedantic wisdom and selfless service for decades.

Mataji conducted Jnana Yagnas (spiritual discourses) not only in Andhra Pradesh but also in other states during her Brahmacharini days. Today, Chinmayaranyam has grown into a vibrant centre encompassing spiritual practices, educational initiatives through Harihara Vidyalaya, and programs like Bala Vihar and Chinmaya Yuva Kendra (CHYK) that touch the lives of thousands.
