Our Activities

Serving Through Devotion & Action

Chinmayaranyam is a vibrant centre of spiritual and social activity. Rooted in the teachings of Pujya Gurudev Swami Chinmayananda, our ashram serves as a lighthouse of Vedantic wisdom, providing a nurturing environment for seekers, students, and the community.

Our activities span across two major wings — the Ashramam for spiritual practices and seva, and Harihara Vidyalaya for value-based education.

Programs & Outreach

• • •

Bala Vihar

Weekly sessions for children aged 5 to 18, imparting lessons on Hindu culture, values, and philosophy in an engaging and interactive manner.

Chinmaya Yuva Kendra

CHYK — a youth wing for individuals aged 18 to 30, focusing on harnessing youth potential through dynamic spirituality and leadership.

Annadanam

Free food distribution to devotees, pilgrims, and the underprivileged, embodying the spirit of selfless service.

Welfare Programs

Community welfare initiatives including health camps, skill development, and support for the underprivileged.

Educational Support

Scholarships, study materials, and mentoring for deserving students from economically weaker sections.
