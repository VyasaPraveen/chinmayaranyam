How to Reach Us

• • •

By Train

Nearest railway station: Tirupati Junction. The ashram is accessible by auto-rickshaw or taxi from the station.

By Air

Nearest airport: Tirupati Airport (TIR). Regular flights from major cities. Taxis available at the airport.

By Road

Well connected via NH roads from Chennai, Bangalore, and Hyderabad. APSRTC buses available to Tirupati.
