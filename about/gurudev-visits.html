Divine Grace

Pujya Gurudev Swami Chinmayananda

Pujya Gurudev Swami Chinmayananda (1916–1993) was one of the most influential spiritual leaders of the 20th century. Through his dynamic exposition of Vedantic philosophy and his charismatic personality, he inspired millions worldwide to pursue a life of purpose, devotion, and service.

His visits to Chinmayaranyam were moments of immense spiritual significance, where he blessed the land, guided the establishment of the ashram, and inspired the community with his wisdom and vision for a spiritually awakened society.

"What you have is His gift to you. What you do with what you have is your gift to Him."

— Swami Chinmayananda

Sacred Visits to Chinmayaranyam

• • •
First Visit

Blessing the Sacred Land

Pujya Gurudev's first visit to the Chinmayaranyam site was a momentous occasion. He personally selected and blessed the land near Tirupati, recognizing its spiritual potential as a place where Vedantic wisdom could flourish alongside the ancient temple traditions of the region.

Inauguration

Laying the Foundation

During the inauguration of the ashram, Gurudev performed the Bhoomi Puja and laid the cornerstone for the main temple and prayer hall. He shared his vision for the ashram to become a centre of spiritual education and selfless service for the people of Andhra Pradesh.

Jnana Yagna

Spreading the Light of Knowledge

Gurudev conducted spiritual discourses (Jnana Yagnas) at Chinmayaranyam, drawing thousands of seekers from across the region. His powerful exposition of the Bhagavad Gita and Upanishads inspired many to take up the path of spiritual study and selfless action.
